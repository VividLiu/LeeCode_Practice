105. Given two integer arrays preorder and inorder where preorder is the preorder traversal of a binary tree and inorder is the inorder traversal of the same tree, construct and return the binary tree.


Example 1: 
        3
     /     \
    9       20
           /  \
         15    7

Input: preorder = [3,9,20,15,7], inorder = [9,3,15,20,7]
Output: [3,9,20,null,null,15,7]

Example 2: 
Input: preorder = [-1], inorder = [-1]
Output: [-1]
